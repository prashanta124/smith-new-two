About

Academic profile

Dr Smith Mehta is an Assistant Professor at the University of Groningen. His work examines digital media industries, platform cultures, creator economies, media policy, and Global South audiovisual industries.

Teaching

Courses & supervision

He teaches and coordinates courses in media platforms, audiovisual cultures, digital ethnography, media spaces and practices, and supervises BA and MA theses.

Conferences

Talks and panels

Selected presentations include AoIR, ICA, ECREA, BASAS, Oxford Internet Institute workshops, and invited book talks across India and internationally.

Media & Public Work

Public scholarship

His public writing and commentary appear in outlets including Indian Express, 360info, Newslaundry, Bloomberg, Airmail, Mid-day, and New Books Network.
